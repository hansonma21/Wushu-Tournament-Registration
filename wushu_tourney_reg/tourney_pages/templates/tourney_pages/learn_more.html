General Information

The 6th Ohio International Chinese Martial Arts Tournament will be held on Saturday, August 3rd, 2024 at The Mintonette Sports Facility. This event is open to all styles of Chinese Martial Arts as well as all skill levels and ages.

Contact: ohiowushuacademy@gmail.com

Important Deadlines

  • Early Registration Closes: July 17th, 2024
  • Registration Closes: July 17th, 2024
  • Virtual Competition Video Submission Closes: August 3rd, 2024

Competition Rules

All other rules will be the same as last year. We will be using the US Wushu Union Rules, 2004 USWU Addendum, and IWUF Rules for all events.
